AutoInspect - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

AutoInspect ("we", "our", "the app") is a vehicle inspection application developed by ARCHIGENIUS, UNIPESSOAL LDA (NIF: 519232526), based in Porto, Portugal. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data stored locally on your device:

  • Inspection data: Vehicle information (make, model, year, VIN, license plate), inspection results, AI analysis, checklists, and severity assessments.
  • Photos: Images captured during inspections are stored locally on your device.
  • Preferences: App settings, paywall state, and onboarding status.

2.2 Data sent to external services:

  • AI Analysis: Vehicle photos and inspection data are sent to our AI proxy server for damage analysis and report generation. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Purchase data: In-app purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number).
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs.
  • We do not share data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Camera Permission

The app requests camera access solely to capture photos during vehicle inspections. Photos are stored locally on your device and are only sent to our AI service when you explicitly request an analysis.

5. Internet Permission

Internet access is used for:

  • Sending inspection data and photos to our AI proxy for analysis.
  • Processing in-app purchases through Google Play.

6. Data Storage and Security

All inspection data is stored locally in an encrypted Room database on your device. We do not maintain cloud backups of your data. The app explicitly disables Android cloud backup to protect your privacy.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings screen ("Delete Account & Data"). This action is irreversible and permanently removes all inspections, vehicle profiles, and preferences from your device.

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

AutoInspect is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
